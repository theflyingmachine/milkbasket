ABOUT US

Who are we?

Welcome to Milk Basket, your number one source for Fresh Organic Milk. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of cow milk, with a focus on Quality, Freshness, and Organic.

Founded in 1999 by Abraham Joseph, MilkBasket has come a long way from its beginnings in Raxaul, Bihar. When Abraham Joseph first started out, his passion for starting a dairy farm, drove him to do a lot of hard work so that Milk Basket can offer Fresh & Organic Milk. We now serve customers all over Raxaul City. We are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. And help hundreds of small-scale milk producers all across the country to do digital and manage their billing and accounting easily for the customers.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
